How does the Test Game Work?

Player 1 (Red):
Aim: Hold X
Reverse Orbit: Release X
Fire: C

Player 2 (Blue):
Aim: Hold Semicolon (;)
Reverse Orbit: Release Semicolon
Fire: Slash (/)

The Test Game is a two player game where you try shoot your opponent three times with your lasers.
Each player has a triangle, or "pointer" orbiting them.
"Aim" slows down you pointer, and "Reverse Orbit" reverses the orbit direction of the pointer.
When you click "Fire", a tracer will come out from the player in the direction of the pointer.
A laser follows shortly after. If you are hit by your enemy's laser you take 1 damage.
Take three damage and you lose.
